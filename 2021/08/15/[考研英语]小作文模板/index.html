考研英语小作文模板

Raffi Zheng
2021-08-15
English

小作文分为两种：

  1. 书信
  2. 通知

书信框架

称呼：
        开头段：自我介绍/寒暄+写信目的
        中间段：分层描述
        结尾段：重申写作目的+期盼回复
                                礼貌语，
                                署名

称呼

  1. Dear + 人名/昵称,
  2. Dear + Mr./Ms. + 姓氏,
  3. Dear + 头衔/职位 + 姓名,
  4. Dear + 集体身份,
  5. Dear Sir or Madam,

自我介绍

  1. 学生：I am a freshman/sophomore/junior/senior/undergraduate/graduate/postgraduate in the Department of Art in 某大学.
  2. 学生团体：I am the president of the Students’ Union of 某大学.
  3. 公司职员：I am a staff member from your company.
  4. 社会人士：I am sb who + 定从/ As sb who +定从.

寒暄

  1. 好久不见：Long time no see!/ It has been a long time since last time we met.
  2. 最近好吗：How is everything going?/ How have you been?
  3. 收到信后很开心：I am exceedingly delighted to receive your last letter (in which you mentioned that …).

写信目的

必写：I’m writing for the purpose of doing sth …

  1. 求职：apply for the postion of …
  2. 推荐：heartily recommend … as your proper choice
  3. 投诉：complain about …/ makea complaint about …
  4. 邀请：invite you to …
  5. 资讯：request for some detailed information about …
  6. 感谢/道歉：express my heartfelt gratitude/ apology for …
  7. 建议：offer/ give/ propose you some advice/ suggestions on …
  8. 辞职：resign from the current position

分层描述

　(自己写内容，要精彩)
　To begin with,…In addition,…Finally,…

期待回复

　 I would appreciate it if you would send me a reply at your earliest convenience.

通知框架

        Notice
                    Dec.18,2021
P1：目的+活动介绍+时间
P2：具体信息点
P3：期待参与+联系方式
                    署名

框架例文

Notice
Dec.18,2021
　　Volunteers are needed for an international conference on globalization next month in our school libary.
　　First of all, ... What's more, ... Last but not the least, ...
　　If you are interested in this activity, please contact us by calling 12345 or sending your resume to 12345@qq.com in one week. Welcome to come and expect your excellent performance.
Postgraduate's Association
